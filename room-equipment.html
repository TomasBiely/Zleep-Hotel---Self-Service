Room Equipment



Each room has been designed in a timeless and minimalistic Scandinavian style and include the following:


  • Worktable and chair
  • TV
  • Pillows
  • Duvets
  • Bed linen
  • Clothes hangers
  • Toiletries
  • Hair dryer
