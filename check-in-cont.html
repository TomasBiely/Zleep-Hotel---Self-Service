Check In / Out



Self-check-in machine is in the reception area and is available for visitors during and out of reception opening times.


The reception team opening times:

  • Monday to Friday:     6:00 – 21:00
  • Weekends:                 7:30 – 21:00

Check-in


We are open to guests for check-in all days from 2pm. To make the check-in process smooth and efficient, we encourage all our guests to use the self-check-in machines located in the reception area. Watch the video guide for the self-check-in machine here.

Check-out


We ask all our guests to check out no later than 11am.

Simply leave your door card in the key box at the reception when you leave. Invoice for your stay will be automatically sent to the email address provided during the reservation.

  • We can accommodate a late check-out until 1pm for an additional 100 DKK. Please let reception know if you would like this service.

Additional or lost key card


We provide 1 key card per room booking.

Additional or replacement key card can be purchased using the self-check-in machine or at the reception for additional 50 DKK.

Watch the video guide for the self-check-in machine here.
