Parking


FREE PARKING


We offer free parking during your stay at the hotel. See here the map of available parking options.



CAR REGISTRATION

You must register your car on arrival using the parking machine located in the reception area. Each registration is valid for 96 hrs. If you are staying with us longer, please ensure you repeat the registration process before the 96hrs are up.


Have the following details ready when using the vehicle registration machine:

  • Vehicle registration number
  • E-mail address
  • Phone number

CHARGING STATION

The nearest charging station for electric vehicles is at AURA Installation.
